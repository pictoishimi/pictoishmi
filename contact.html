Frequently Asked Questions

How long are the consultation calls?

Initial consultation calls are typically 30 minutes. For more complex projects, we can schedule a longer follow-up session.

What should I prepare for the call?

Think about your goals, target audience, and any specific challenges you're facing. This will help us provide the most value during our conversation.

Can I reschedule my call?

Yes, you can reschedule up to 24 hours before your scheduled call time. Use the rescheduling section above to change your appointment.

What happens after the call?

We'll send a follow-up email with a summary of our discussion and any next steps or proposals if applicable.
