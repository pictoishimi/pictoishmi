Our Mission

Kandy Fresh Fruit Supplier needed to establish a strong digital presence to compete in the B2B market. Our goal was to position them as the premium fruit supplier for hotels, restaurants, and retailers across Sri Lanka.

We developed a comprehensive digital strategy focused on showcasing their product quality, reliability, and wide variety of fruits. Through professional photography, engaging content, and targeted social media campaigns, we highlighted their unique selling points.

The strategy resulted in a 65% increase in B2B inquiries and established Kandy Fresh as a trusted name in the fruit supply industry.
