Our Strategic Process

1

Discovery & Market Analysis

Comprehensive analysis of UK furniture retail landscape, target audience preferences, and competitor social media strategies. Identifying opportunities in sustainable, story-driven content.

2

Brand Positioning Strategy

Developing brand pillars focusing on craftsmanship, sustainability, and lifestyle integration. Creating visual guidelines that emphasize natural materials and aspirational home styling.

3

Content Strategy Development

Creating content pillars around craftsmanship stories, sustainable practices, home styling tips, and customer transformations. Planning consistent posting schedule across platforms.

4

Community Engagement Plan

Building engaged community through interactive content, user-generated features, and responsive communication. Establishing brand as authority in sustainable furniture space.
