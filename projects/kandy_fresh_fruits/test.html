Strategy & Visual Identity

Connecting the freshness of the farm directly to the consumer through visual storytelling.

The Challenge

Kandy Fresh Fruit needed to differentiate themselves in a crowded market. They had the best produce, but their online presence felt generic. Our goal was to make "Freshness" something you could almost taste through the screen.

We focused on high-contrast imagery, behind-the-scenes content from the sourcing process, and highlighting the speed of delivery. This built trust with both household buyers and restaurant partners.

Result: A visual identity that positions Kandy Fresh Fruit as the premium choice for health-conscious consumers.
