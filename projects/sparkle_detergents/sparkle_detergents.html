Our Strategy & Brand Identity

Our Mission

Our goal with Sparkle was to refresh the brand's social presence and make it feel modern, clean, and consistent. Before we stepped in, their content was irregular and didn't fully highlight how strong the products actually were.

We created a simple but effective social strategy built around bright product visuals, relatable cleaning moments, and clear product benefits. Every month, we planned a mix of product showcases, cleaning tips, and lifestyle shots to keep the brand active and easy to engage with.

This approach helped Sparkle show up professionally across platforms, reach more people, and build a steady, recognisable online presence.
